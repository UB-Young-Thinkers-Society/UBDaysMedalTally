System Overview

HTML
Structure
Clean, accessible layouts align with event workflows and results display.
Semantic elements and consistent sections keep the experience intuitive.
CSS
Style
Responsive design, subtle shadows, and animations for clarity and delight.
Color scheme follows UB branding; components maintain uniform spacing.
JS
Interaction
Snappy interactions power filters, reveals, and smooth transitions.
Lightweight scripts drive real-time feedback and micro-animations.
SB
Database & Auth
Secure storage of events, roles, and live medal counts.

Hosting
Fast, reliable delivery of pages and assets during UB Days.
Vercel ships updates seamlessly, ensuring high availability.

Collaborators

This project is a collaboration between the University of Bohol and the Student / Personnel Services (SPS) office. Together, we aim to foster fairness, innovation, and community engagement throughout UB Days.

We extend our gratitude to all stakeholders whose support and mission alignment make this system possible.
