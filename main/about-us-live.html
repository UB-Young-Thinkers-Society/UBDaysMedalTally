University of Bohol Young Thinkers Society

The official student-led organization of the Computer Science department.

We are passionate about using technology to create innovative solutions and foster a community of learning. This real-time medal tally system is one of our projects, built to bring transparency and modern data tracking to the UB Days celebration.

UB DAYS MEDAL TALLY 2025

This project is a collaboration between the University of Bohol and the Student Personnel Services (SPS) office. Together, we aim to foster fairness, innovation, and community engagement throughout UB Days.

We extend our gratitude to all stakeholders whose support and mission alignment make this system possible.
